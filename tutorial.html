How to Play

This game is pretty simple. You and the enemy take turns drawing and playing cards. The goal is not to die first. That’s it.

Basic Stuff

  • You start with 5 cards.
  • You play 1 card per turn.
  • Then the enemy does something probably annoying.
  • If you die, you lose. If enemy dies, you win. Very complicated system.

Card Types (important-ish)

Attack cards - They hurt things. Usually the enemy. Sometimes you.

Defense cards - They stop you from instantly dying (sometimes).

Death cards - These are not friendly. Don’t trust them.

Chaos cards - Nobody understands them, not even me.

Weird Rules You Should Know

  • Melatonin: if you use it 3 times in a row, you die. Don’t ask why.
  • Cheese: it is moldy and does random things. That’s the entire explanation.
  • Pipe Bomb: it is exactly what it sounds like.

Final Thing

Don’t overthink it. Just play cards and hope Digo doesn’t show up.
