How to Play

This game is simple. You and the enemy take turns drawing and playing cards. The goal is to survive longer than them. Or just not die instantly. Same thing.

Basic Rules

  • You start with 5 cards.
  • You play 1 card per turn.
  • Then the enemy plays something that will probably ruin your day.
  • If you die, you lose. If enemy dies, you win. Life is simple like that.

Card Types

Attack cards — They hurt things. Usually the enemy. Sometimes reality itself.

Defense cards — They stop death. Not always. But sometimes. Trust issues included.

Death cards — If you see these, start praying or accept fate.

Chaos cards — Nobody knows what they do. Including me.

Important Chaos Rules

  • Melatonin: 3 uses in a row = you fall asleep permanently (don’t ask questions).
  • Cheese: moldy, unstable, emotionally unpredictable.
  • Pipe Bomb: self-explanatory. If it exists, it is already a problem.

Final Message

Don’t think too hard. Just play cards, survive Digo, and pretend everything is normal.
