How to Play

This is a turn-based card game. You and the enemy take turns drawing and playing cards. The goal is to survive longer than your opponent.

Rules

  • You start with 5 cards.
  • Each turn, you play one card.
  • After your turn, the enemy plays.
  • If you die or run out of cards, you lose.

Card Types

Attack - Damage enemy or trigger effects.

Defense - Blocks death effects.

Death - Kills instantly unless blocked.

Chaos - Random effects.

Special Rules

  • Melatonin: 3 in a row = overdose and death.
  • Cheese: random effect each use.
  • Pipe Bomb: attacks enemy directly.
