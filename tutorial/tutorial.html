How to Play

This game is simple. Your emotions will not be.

Basic Rules

  • You start with 5 cards.
  • Play 1 card per turn.
  • Enemy plays after you.
  • If you die, you lose. If enemy dies, you win.

Card Types (Full Explanation)

Attack Cards

These are your damage cards. They are meant to hurt the enemy, but sometimes they feel like they are also hurting your mental stability.

Example: Pipe Bomb — instantly ends enemy if not blocked.

Defense Cards

These stop death effects. They are basically emotional support items that sometimes work.

Example: Package Defuse — cancels one death card.

Death Cards

These cards end your run instantly unless you have defense. If you draw one, you are legally required to panic.

Examples: Digo, Lego Brick

Chance Death Cards

These cards are gambling with your existence. Sometimes you survive. Sometimes you uninstall life.

Example: Sus USB — 50/50 survival.

Chaos Cards

These do random things. Nobody understands them. Not even the developer (me).

Examples:

  • Radioactive Goose — triggers random chaos effect
  • U-Haul — swaps turn order or causes accidents
  • Crazy Pokeball — weird RNG defense interaction

Special Cards

  • Check — gives you extra cards (money is fake anyway)
  • Cheese — mold-based random effects (could heal, could kill, could betray you)
  • Melatonin — if used 3 times total, you overdose and lose the game
  • Wet Fish — stuns enemy (emotionally and physically)

Final Warning

If you are confused, that is normal.

If Digo appears in your game, do not trust it.

If you lose to a cheese card, that is also normal.
