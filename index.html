Lore of Digo

Here’s some lore about my mascot — Digo.

Digo was an amazing dino who ate all his trees and carrots so that his parents would stop telling him about how kids in Africa couldn’t eat because he didn’t finish his food.

One day, a duck from that one song I can’t seem to remember waddled up to Digo and said: "got any uranium?"

Out of nowhere, Digo was hit from behind by a goose carrying uranium.

He was now a dino-goose mutant, hence his name: Digo.
