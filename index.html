Digo's lore

Digo was an amazing dino who ate all his trees and carrots so his parents would stop talking about starving children.

One day, a duck from that one song i cant seem to remember walked up to him and asked : "got any uranium?"

Before Digo could answer, a radioactive goose sprinted directly into his spine.

Digo became a horrifying goose-dinosaur mutant. Hence his named - Digo.

Nobody knows why he carries USB sticks now. Just accept fate if you see him
